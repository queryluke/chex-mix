Preheat oven to 275°F

In a small saucepan, over medium heat, melt butter and jalapeños until the peppers are soft and have released their juices (about 4 minutes).

In a second small saucepan, over medium-high heat, add 1/4c milk and flour. Stir well. When mixture thickens add the rest of the milk and seasoned salt. Turn the heat down to medium-low and reduce for 3-4 minutes. Add the beer, cheddar, and hot sauce. Stir until combined.

In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients. Strain the butter mixture into the dry ingredients and mix well.

Split between 2 large, glass baking dishes. Pour the cheese sauce over and stir. Cook for 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes.

Spread out on paper towels to cool.
