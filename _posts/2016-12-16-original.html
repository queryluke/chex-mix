Preheat oven to 250°F

Melt butter in a small bowl and mix in seasoned salt, Worcestershire, and onion powder.

In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients. Pour in the butter mixture, and mix well.

Split between 2 large, glass baking dishes and cook for 30 minutes, stirring every 10 minutes.

Spread out on paper towels to cool.
